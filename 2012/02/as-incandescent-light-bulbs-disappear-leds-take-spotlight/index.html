As incandescent light bulbs disappear, LEDs take spotlight

Tweet

Video Story
By Krystal Klei

Monday, Feb. 20, 2012

Traditional light bulbs are being phased out in the U.S., leaving consumers and businesses with the choice of using compact fluorescent or LED replacements. The switch, brought on by the need to save energy, presents a major business opportunity for bulb makers. Cronkite News reporter Krystal Klei has the story.
