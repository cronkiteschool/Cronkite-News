Correction to Cronkite News story on Nile crocodiles

Monday, Sept. 16, 2013

PHOENIX – A Sept. 9 Cronkite News story on the birth of rare Nile crocodiles erroneously reported the maximum adult weight of the species. That figure is 1,500 pounds.
