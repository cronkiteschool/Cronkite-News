Interest in alternative medicine remains high among Americans

Tweet

Video Story
By Taylor Bryant

Monday, April 14, 2014

Breath in, breathe out. Modern medicine might have you puffing for a stethoscope, but research has found that 38 percent Americans prefer holistic healing methods. Instead of opting first for medication, many are choosing aroma therapy, acupuncture and yoga. Cronkite News reporter Taylor Bryant has the story.
